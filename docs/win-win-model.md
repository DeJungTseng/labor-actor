# Win-Win Model

Labor-actor is designed as a non-zero-sum system.

## Workers
- Immediate emotional stabilization
- Clear understanding of rights and options
- Access to real human support
- Reduced shame in asking for help

## Labor Unions
- Increased visibility
- Access to members who genuinely need help
- Better-prepared cases

## Labor Lawyers
- Structured, relevant case leads
- Reduced emotional triage burden
- Increased professional visibility

## Mental Health Support Organizations
- Proper crisis routing
- Reduced overload from non-clinical cases
- Earlier intervention opportunities

Labor-actor does not replace any role.
It **connects them at the right time**.
